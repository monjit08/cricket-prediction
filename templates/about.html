WHAT IS THIS WEBSITE ABOUT?

Cricology, our website, is a one-of-a-kind website that aims to make cricket, the gentleman's game, more technologically advanced and more engrossing too . This website is your one-stop-cricket destination that provides you with all kinds of info on the upcoming IPL 2020 . It predict real-time scores and can also predict the winner of the match even before the game starts . This website also provides engrossing information and statistics of the past played IPL seasons . We have tons and tons of features that keeps you engrossed this cricket-season.
